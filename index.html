More than 70% of people suffer from circadian disruption. This is linked to...

Sleep Problems

Inconsistent circadian rhythm is frequently linked to problems falling asleep, waking up, and being overly sleepy during the day.

Low Energy

Disorders of the circadian rhythm are linked to decreased cellular energy generation and tiredness.

Weight Gain

Obesity, insulin resistance, and metabolic diseases are all strongly influenced by an irregular circadian cycle.

Low Mood

Bipolar disorder, seasonal affective disorder, bipolar disorder, and depression are all frequently exacerbated by circadian disruption.

Aging

While a healthy circadian rhythm is essential to durability, an out-of-sync circadian rhythm is linked to aging and morbidity.

+ Every modern disease

From cardiovascular disorders to cancer. Sounds incredible? Just Google “circadian rhythm” + any ailment.
